# Project Title
Phishing Awareness and Cybersecurity Training Materials

## Overview
This project focuses on developing cybersecurity awareness training materials to help users identify phishing emails. It was created as part of a university cybersecurity initiative aimed at improving security awareness among students, staff, and faculty.

The materials are designed to strengthen understanding of social engineering attacks and improve the ability to recognize suspicious email behavior in real-world scenarios.

## Objective
The primary goal of this project is to improve cybersecurity awareness by:
- Educating users on how phishing attacks operate
- Demonstrating differences between legitimate and malicious emails
- Reducing the risk of social engineering attacks through training materials
- Supporting cybersecurity awareness initiatives in an academic environment

## Key Topics Covered
- Phishing email identification
- Social engineering awareness
- Email based attack patterns
- Cybersecurity best practices for end users

## Key Responsibilities
- Developed educational materials illustrating phishing vs legitimate email patterns
- Created training content for students and staff focused on email security awareness
- Collaborated on faculty-focused cybersecurity awareness materials
- Supported cybersecurity education initiatives through example-based learning content

## Content Included
This repository includes:
- Examples of phishing emails commonly aimed at university students
- Examples of phishing emails commonly aimed at university staff
- Student training material
- Staff training material

## Key Learning Outcomes
- Understanding of phishing attack structures and patterns
- Improved ability to distinguish phishing vs legitimate emails
- Experience in cybersecurity awareness training development
- Exposure to real-world social engineering concepts

## Future Improvements

- Adding interactive training modules
- Incorporating real-time phishing simulation exercises
